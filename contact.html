FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
+
We support small-batch customization. Most products start from 50–100 pcs. The exact MOQ depends on product type and process — feel free to inquire.
How long is the delivery lead time?
+
Standard orders take 7–15 business days. Complex processes or bulk orders require 15–25 business days. We will confirm the exact delivery date before production.
Can I get samples?
+
Yes, we offer sampling services. The sampling fee varies by product complexity and can be deducted from the bulk order once confirmed.
What printing processes do you support?
+
We support offset printing, digital printing, screen printing, foil stamping (gold/silver), embossing/debossing, UV coating, lamination, and more. Choose based on your budget and desired effect.
How should I provide design files?
+
Please provide vector source files in AI, CDR, or PDF format, or high-resolution (300dpi+) JPG/PNG images. We also offer paid design support if needed.
Do you ship internationally?
+
Yes, we can arrange international express or sea freight. Please specify the destination when inquiring for accurate shipping costs and timelines.
